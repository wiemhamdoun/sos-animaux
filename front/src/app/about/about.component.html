What we do

Our Mission

1. To rescue animals in dire need
2. To provide quality veterinary care & rehabilitation
3. To stabilise the stray dog & cat population through spay/neutering programs
4. To eradicate rabies, creating a healthier environment for humans and animals
5. To provide education & adoption programmes

Our story

Anipat is an animal service foundation founded in 2011. Our mission is to ensure pets stay in loving homes by using a multi-step approach.

Our story

Anipat supports TNR (trap, neuter, return) for free-roaming cats. We helped implement the first ever Free-Roaming Cat Program in Columbus and support the current Community Cat Program. And we strive to educate the community concerning animal welfare and responsible ownership.

Our story

We are excited to be helping animals and the people who share their lives with them by our services and outreach.
